Services

Custom enterprise-level web development

I specialize in functional web design and development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and various JavaScript libraries, prioritizing responsiveness and a mobile-first approach. I also develop the middle-tier interface to efficiently gather and store data using XML, JSON, PHP and SQL. I create websites and web applications that provide the optimal solution for clients and give an efficient end-user experience.

Budget-friendly website design and implementation

For local businesses who may not have the time or resources to get a website up and running quickly, I build fast, beautiful, responsive websites using online website builders. One of the key elements to a successful website is for it to be well-organized with content that is easy for users to find and absorb quickly. We discuss your goals, style that suits your business, and content. I provide a mock up for discussion and engage in iterative feedback sessions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the end result.

Graphic design for social media, websites, and print

I provide creative design solutions for printed materials such as brochures, mailers, postcards, invitations and window clings. I create digital graphics for social media that are simple and effective to convey your message to your customers.
